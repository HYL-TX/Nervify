STATION 01 — THE MOTOR UNIT

      
A command travels from nerve to muscle.

      

        A motor neuron fires an action potential down its axon. At the neuromuscular
        junction the signal crosses to the muscle fibre, which contracts. EMG records
        the command; the load cell records the force. MyoForce captures both.
      

    

  


  

    

      
STATION 02 — ONE RATIO

      
Efficiency is force per unit of effort.

      

        Both signals are normalised to the session's own maximum, then divided. A higher
        ratio means more force for the same electrical activity — a hallmark of healthy
        motor control, and a target of rehabilitation.
      

      
NME = %MVC force ÷ %MVC EMG

    

  


  

    

      
STATION 03 — RECOVERY

      
Across sessions, a recovery curve.

      

        No population norm exists for thumb NME, so progress is read against the patient's
        own previous session. Session after session, the trend tells the recovery story —
        exported to a per-patient report.
